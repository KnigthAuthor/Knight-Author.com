About the Author

Sharing the spark of creative writing after ignoring the stories in my head for years. In 2022 I began to build a fictional world based on inspiration from my love of Science Fiction and Fantasy stories, games and movies. During this process I have also been writing short stories based on communities I've been a part of who have encouraged me during my writing journey. I never know where the sparks will lead until I've finished writing, no matter what plan I set in place. Join me in following the sparks as they fly through the stars, taking us to futuristic landscapes and adventures.
